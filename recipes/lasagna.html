Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is one of the best pastas I have ever put into my mouth! If you haven't tried it, you don't know what you're missing! It's easy to make and is great for the whole family! Perfect with Texas Toast!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Swiss Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Lay down a layer of noodles in a pan
  3. Add a layer of sauce, then cottage cheese, and then both cheddar and swiss cheese
  4. Repeat this process until you have three layers of lasagna made
  5. Cook for 30 minutes and serve!
